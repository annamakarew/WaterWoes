Our Mission

Our oceans are one of the most important bodies on our planet. Integral to all forms of life, the oceans provide habitats to a diverse array of species, are a part of the carbon cycle, and influence weather and climate patterns. Our ocean takes up 70% of the the world's surface area and inhabit an estimated amount of 2 million species. This large body of saltwater is a huge part of the carbon cycle and stores much of the carbon put into the atmosphere. The ocean's currents transport winds, warm waters, and precipitaion. They play a large part in regulating climate.

As a result of climate change and pollution, our oceans are threatened. Because of climate change, the oceans are warming and sea levels are rising. As a result, the once vibrant and coloful coral reefs are being bleached. Through our overdependence of plastic, all the waste ends up in the ocean and threatens species and pollutes water. As humans, we have a responsibility to keep clean our oceans and beautiful. We want to foster a passion and urge to save our oceans through this website.
